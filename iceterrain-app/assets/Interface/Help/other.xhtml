Other Stuff

Options

 The options window lets you change various settings which affect performance and visual quality. If it's running slow for you, or you wish to see what the terrain looks like with other settings, play with stuff in here.

Video Recording

 Press F12 to toggle recording MJPEG videos to HOME%/Documents/Iceterrain/Videos. This file is raw JPEG images, so should be processed in a video tool before uploading (it can get pretty big).
