Opening Terrain

 First you will need a terrain template to work on. Click on Terrain button to list all available templates. Currently only 'Blend' is available. Once selected, you will be offered to Clone the tempate, or open it Read Only. If you choose to clone, the entire terrain will be downloaded to your computer, and you will be working on the copy. Your projects can be found in %HOME%/Documents/Iceterrain.
