Editing Terrain

Select Mode

Shows general operations for the current tile. More will be added here soon.

Paint Mode

Lets you raise or lower the terrain. Click once to raise or lower by the current amount, or click and hold to repeat. Press Ctrl to reverse the brush, i.e. paint becomes erase, erase becomes paint.

Flatten Mode

Click once to set the elevation you want to flatten too, then click and drag to paint the flattened terrain.

Splat Mode

Lets you paint the 4 textures allowed per tile. You can choose the texture image, or select it as the current brush, as well as edit clutter definitions for that texture. Click once to paint, or click and hold to repeat.

Liquid Plane

Choose a water plane, then use the spinner to move the elevation up and down.

Options

The options window lets you change various settings which affect performance and visual quality. If it's running slow for you, or you wish to see what the terrain looks like with other settings, play with stuff in here.

Other Goodies

Press F12 to toggle recording MJPEG videos to HOME%/Documents/Iceterrain/Videos (probably will need processing in an editor before uploading).
